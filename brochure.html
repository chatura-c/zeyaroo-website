Platform Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

One professional home for every client engagement - from first message to final payment.

Structured Proposals

Send versioned proposals with clear deliverables, timelines, and milestones. Counter-propose and negotiate in one place.

Milestone Escrow

Clients fund each milestone before work begins. Payments are protected and released on completion - automatically.

Protected Media Delivery

Share watermarked previews instantly. Full-resolution files unlock only when the milestone is approved.

Client Portal

Clients get a clean portal to view proposals, fund milestones, and download approved media. Professional by default.

Creator Profile

A professional public presence with portfolio, bio, service categories, and social links - not just another feed.

Shareable Galleries

Generate password-protected, expiring gallery links. Share selects with clients or publish a portfolio - your call.

Process

Simple from first message
to final payment

1

Send a Proposal

Define deliverables, timeline, and milestones. Negotiate terms inline until both parties agree - everything versioned.

2

Client Funds Milestones

Funds are held in escrow before work begins. Your payment is secured before you pick up the camera.

3

Deliver & Get Paid

Upload media, share watermarked previews, release full files on approval. Funds transfer on completion.
